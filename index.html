About

CoinToss is a nice heads or tails simulator on iOS. It comes with multiple coins, an awesome wood background & some silly sounds. The RNG is reasonably random, using proper srand() and rand().

Features

Throw Gesture
Just shake your phone as if you were flipping pancakes. Realistic UX for the win!
Select coins
Slide the table to the right to reveal alternative coins and some settings:
Settings
Toggle Sound, Vibration & Throw Gesture recognition
